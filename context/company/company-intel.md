## Organizational Signals

- **Research-heavy composition:** Founding team is 4 academics/researchers. Team likely skews heavily toward ML research and engineering.
- **Product function being built out:** A Product Operations function is actively being stood up in 2026. The product org is early and still taking shape, so process and operating cadence are being established rather than inherited.
- **Small team, big surface area:** ~50-60 people managing a consumer app (Marble), developer API, and enterprise partnerships. Resources are constrained relative to the surface area.

## Culture Indicators
