# Hiring Signals — World Labs

## Product Operations Function (mid-2026)

As of mid-2026, World Labs is actively building a dedicated **Product Operations** function, including a tactical Product Operations role on the Marble team. This is the current truth: the product org has moved from "to be defined" toward standing up an operating function. The Product Operations scope is tactical and execution-facing: burndown, launch readiness, design-partner onboarding, and operating cadence.

This reframes how to read the older role analysis below: company-wide product strategy is no longer wide open, and the immediate hiring need centers on operational execution that supports an established product direction.

### Implied First 90 Days

For a tactical Product Operations role on the Marble team:
1. **Weeks 1-4:** Learn the technology and Marble surface, meet the team, map the current operating cadence and in-flight launches
2. **Weeks 5-8:** Stand up the operating mechanics: burndown, launch-readiness checklist, design-partner onboarding flow
3. **Weeks 9-12:** Run a launch end to end on the new cadence; tighten the metrics and reporting framework
