# Marble App — Consumer Product

**URL:** [marble.worldlabs.ai](https://marble.worldlabs.ai)

## Overview

Marble is World Labs' consumer-facing product for generating explorable 3D worlds from text prompts and images. Users type a description or upload an image, and Marble generates a persistent, navigable 3D environment. The current models are Marble 1.1 and Marble 1.1 Plus (released 2026-04-02). Marble 1.1 is the default model at 1,500 credits per world. Marble 1.1 Plus adds automatic spatial expansion for larger, more complex worlds at variable pricing per world.

## Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Price | Credits | Key Features |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Free** | $0 | 7,000 | Basic generation (text / image /360 pano), 4 worlds |
| **Stanard** | ~$20/mo | 20K | Multi-image input, Video input, Advanced create flow with editing, "Chisel" mode for 3D layout control, Export splat files, Export 360 panoramas, Export low-resolution collider meshes, Community asset downloads, Purchase top-up credits |
| **Pro** | ~$35/mo | 40K | Expand worlds to larger spaces, Enhance rendered video outputs, export high-resolution textured meshes, Commercial rights |
| **Premium** | ~$95/mo | 120K | Everything + 75 world generations |

## Key Capabilities

- **Text → World:** Describe a scene, get a navigable 3D environment
- **Image → World:** Upload a photo or artwork, generate the 3D world it implies
- **Persistent worlds:** Generated worlds are saved and shareable via URL
- **Editing:** Generated worlds can be modified. Advanced editing surfaces under the "Create & edit" option in the omnibox, with Chisel for 3D layout control plus Marble Studio
- **Navigation:** First-person exploration of generated environments
- **Sharing:** Public URLs for each generated world 

## Strengths

- **Quality:** Generated worlds have high visual fidelity — competitive with game-engine environments
- **Persistence:** Unlike competitors (e.g., Google Genie 3), worlds are saved and can be revisited
- **Accessibility:** No technical knowledge required — type a prompt and explore

## Weaknesses / Gaps 

- **No collaboration:** Single-user experience. No multiplayer or shared exploration.
- **Limited export:** Consumer tier likely doesn't include mesh/splat export
- **No animation:** Worlds are static — no moving objects, weather, or time-of-day changes
- **Discovery:** No gallery, trending worlds, or community features visible yet

## Strategic Role in Portfolio

- **Acquisition engine:** Free tier brings users in, demonstrates the technology
- **Technology validator:** Consumer usage proves the world model works at scale
- **Content marketing:** Beautiful generated worlds serve as viral marketing
- **Data flywheel:** User prompts and usage patterns improve the model
