> All information sourced from publicly available data.

## Use Cases

1. **Concept visualization:** Architects turn sketches into walkable 3D environments in seconds
2. **Interior design:** Designers generate room layouts from mood boards or text descriptions
3. **Real estate staging:** Generate furnished interiors from empty rooms
4. **Urban planning:** Visualize proposed developments in their surroundings
5. **Client presentations:** Generate interactive walkthroughs instead of static renders

## Why It Matters for World Labs

- **Largest partner density:** 4 partners in this vertical (Fenestra, Interior AI, xFigura, SHoP) — most of any vertical
- **Image-to-world is the killer feature:** Architects already work with sketches and reference images — upload and explore is the perfect workflow
- **Consumer + professional market:** Interior AI targets consumers; Fenestra/xFigura target professionals
- **High frequency usage:** Architects iterate constantly — many worlds per project

## Adoption Barriers

- **Precision requirements:** Architecture demands dimensional accuracy — AI generation is approximate
- **Material fidelity:** Architects care about specific materials, finishes, and lighting conditions
- **Existing tool ecosystem:** ArchiCAD, Revit, SketchUp, Rhino have decades of investment
- **Editing depth:** Architects need to modify generated environments, not just view them. Marble now ships editing (Chisel and Marble Studio, surfaced via "Create & edit" in the omnibox), so the open question is editing depth and precision, not whether editing exists
- **Code compliance:** Generated spaces need to respect building codes and accessibility standards
